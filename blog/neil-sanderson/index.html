Neil Sanderson

Posted July 23, 2024

Neil Sanderson is the drummer and backing vocalist, as well as songwriter of Three Days Grace.

Based on the interviews featuring him that I've seen, he's an extraverted person with a good, sometimes a bit rough, sense of humor. I share some of his views on life, regarding people's behavior, motives and issues.

In addition to drumming, he also plays piano and sings really well.
